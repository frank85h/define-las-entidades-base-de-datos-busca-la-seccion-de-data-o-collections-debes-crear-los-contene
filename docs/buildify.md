- Client management (name, address, phone)
- Service scheduling and tracking (date, status, technician, client)
- Equipment registry (brand, model, refrigerant gas, photo)

- Dashboard with feature cards for Clients, Services, and Equipment
- Navigation between main sections
- Stats display for quick overview
